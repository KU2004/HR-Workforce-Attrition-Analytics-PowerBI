# 🚀 HR Workforce Insights & Employee Attrition Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing workforce data and identifying key factors driving employee attrition using an interactive Power BI dashboard. It helps HR teams make data-driven decisions to improve employee retention and workforce planning.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Analyze employee workforce data
* Identify attrition patterns and trends
* Detect high-risk employees
* Support HR decision-making with actionable insights

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** – Dashboard development & data visualization
* **Excel / CSV** – Dataset creation and preprocessing
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – KPI and measure calculations
* **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation

---

## 📊 Dataset Description

The dataset contains 100 employee records with attributes such as:

* Employee ID, Name, Age, Gender
* Department, Job Role, Education
* Monthly Income, Performance Rating
* Job Satisfaction, Work-Life Balance
* Overtime, Work Mode
* Attrition Status & Risk Level

---

## 🔧 Data Preparation (Power Query)

* Removed null and duplicate values
* Converted data types (Date, Numeric, Text)
* Created Age Groups and Experience Bands
* Extracted Year & Month from Joining Date
* Cleaned and standardized categorical data

---

## 🧠 DAX Measures

* Total Employees
* Attrition Count
* Attrition Rate (%)
* Average Monthly Income
* Average Job Satisfaction Score
* Average Work-Life Balance Score
* High Risk Employees Count
* Average Tenure

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 🔝 KPI Cards

* Total Employees
* Attrition Count
* Attrition Rate (%)
* Avg Income
* Avg Satisfaction
* High Risk Employees

### 📊 Visualizations

* Attrition by Department (Bar Chart)
* Attrition by Job Role (Column Chart)
* Attrition Status Distribution (Donut Chart)
* Attrition Trend Over Time (Line Chart)
* Attrition by Work Mode & Overtime (Stacked Bar Chart)
* Workforce Summary (Matrix Table)

### 🎛 Filters (Slicers)

* Department
* Gender
* Job Role
* Work Mode
* Attrition Risk Level

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights

* Identified departments with highest attrition rates
* Found correlation between low satisfaction and higher attrition
* Overtime employees showed higher attrition risk
* High-risk employee groups were highlighted for retention strategies
